# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 2025**

## Overview

Tiny Dude ("the App") is a batch image compression application for macOS. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Data Collection

**Tiny Dude does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data.** All image processing happens entirely on your device.

Specifically:

- **No analytics or tracking** - We do not use any analytics frameworks or tracking tools.
- **No network requests** - The App does not connect to the internet. All compression is performed locally on your Mac.
- **No cloud storage** - Your images are never uploaded to any server. Input and output files remain on your local file system.
- **No personal information** - We do not collect names, emails, device identifiers, or any other personal information.
- **No image data collection** - We do not access, read, or store the content of your images beyond what is necessary to perform the compression you request.

## File Access

Tiny Dude accesses files on your device only when you:

- Drag and drop images into the application
- Use the file picker to select images
- Choose an output folder for compressed images

The App uses macOS App Sandbox, which restricts file access to only the files and folders you explicitly grant permission to.

## EXIF and Metadata

When you enable "Strip metadata," Tiny Dude removes EXIF data (including GPS location, camera settings, and other embedded information) from your compressed images. This is a local operation and no metadata is transmitted anywhere.

## Third-Party Services

Tiny Dude does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

## Children's Privacy

Tiny Dude does not collect any data from any users, including children under the age of 13.
